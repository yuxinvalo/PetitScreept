UK ‘prime destination for money laundering criminals’
Britain remains a “prime destination” for the corrupt and the criminal to spend their illicit wealth, a new report says today.

Transparency International UK states that despite undoubted progress by the government in the fight against corruption Britain remains a country of choice for people laundering billions every year.

“Trillions of pounds flow through the UK financial system each year - inevitably some of these transactions involve corrupt wealth, yet many are given a seal of approval by passing through the UK and its associated territories,” says the report, Paradise Lost.
